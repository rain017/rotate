What are Volleyball Rotations?
Volleyball Rotations determine where each player stands on the court before the ball is served

5-1 Rotations

The 5-1 rotation is a very common rotation used in high level volleyball as there is only one designated setter in charge of setting the ball

6-2 Rotations

This rotation is different from a 5-1 rotation as the setter will always be in the back row. This allows the team to always have 3 hitters in every rotation
